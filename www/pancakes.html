Pancakes

Ingredients for four servings

  • 2 ½ dl flour

  • 2 ½ ml salt

  • 6 dl milk

  • 3 eggs

  • 45 ml butter

Instructions:

Mix flour and salt in a bowl. Beat in half of the milk and whisk to a smooth batter.
Beat in the rest of the milk and the eggs.
Melt the butter in the frying pan and whisk into the batter. Stir thin pancakes of the batter in a frying pan or pancake pan.
