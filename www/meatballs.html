Meatballs

Ingredients for four servings

  • 500 g minced meat

  • ½ dl breadcrumbs

  • 1 dl cream

  • 30 ml finely chopped onion

  • 1 egg

  • 5 ml salt

  • 1 ml black pepper

  • 30 ml Butter- and rapeseed oil

Instructions:

Mix bread and cream. Let swell 10 min. Add the fries, onions, eggs, salt and pepper.
Stir to a smooth smear. Form the batter to even buns. Fry them in butter- and rapeseed oil on medium for 3-5 minutes.
